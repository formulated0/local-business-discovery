Why we support local businesses

Small businesses are the backbone of communities like Parramatta. By giving locals an easy way to discover and review them, we help strengthen the local economy and the connections that make our neighbourhood thrive.

According to the City of Parramatta Council — "Almost 28,000 jobs were added in the last decade, driven by public sector relocations and booming demand for health services."

https://www.cityofparramatta.nsw.gov.au/business/economic-and-industry-insights/economic-insights/local-economic-insights

This website allows us to provide our users with an efficient and effective way of discovering local businesses around Parramatta, subsequently supporting and strengthening local businesses.

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/formulated0/local-business-discovery
